# Overview

This project presents a comprehensive business data analytics solution for a Saudi Arabian retail company using Python. It demonstrates a complete analytics workflow, beginning with raw retail transaction data and progressing through data cleaning, feature engineering, exploratory data analysis (EDA), KPI reporting, and business insights.

The analysis focuses on understanding sales performance, customer behavior, product profitability, regional performance, sales channels, and payment preferences to support data-driven business decisions.

The project was developed in Google Colab using Python and data analytics libraries.

---

# Business Problem

Retail businesses generate large volumes of transactional data every day, but without proper analysis, valuable business opportunities often remain hidden.

Management requires a structured analytical approach to answer critical business questions such as:

- Which products generate the highest sales and profit?
- Which cities and regions perform best?
- How do customers purchase products?
- Which sales channels contribute the most revenue?
- What payment methods are most preferred?
- How can profitability and customer satisfaction be improved?

This project transforms raw operational data into meaningful business insights that support strategic decision-making.

# Key Business Insights

- Generated **SAR 4.37 Million** in total sales.
- Achieved **SAR 1.31 Million** in total profit.
- Maintained an average profit margin of approximately **30%**.
- **Home Appliances** emerged as the highest-performing product category.
- **Refrigerators** generated the highest sales among all products.
- **Dammam** recorded the strongest city-level sales performance.
- The **Western Region** contributed the highest overall revenue.
- **Physical stores** outperformed online channels in total sales.
- **STC Pay** was the most frequently used payment method.
- Customer satisfaction remained strong with an average rating of **4.01/5**.
- Sales and profit demonstrated a strong positive relationship.
